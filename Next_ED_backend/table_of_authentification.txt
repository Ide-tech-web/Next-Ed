Endpoint,Method,Purpose
/api/register/,POST,Creates a new user (via your custom view).
/api/token/,POST,"Login: Takes email and password, returns access_token and refresh_token (JWT built-in)."
/api/token/refresh/,POST,"Takes refresh_token, returns a new access_token (JWT built-in)."
/api/logout/,POST,Takes refresh_token and blacklists it (via your custom view).
